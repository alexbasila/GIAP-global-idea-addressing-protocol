What GIAP does

  • Addressing at birth: assign a permanent, resolvable ID the moment an idea appears.
  • Relevance matching: connect to related ideas, needs, and synergies globally.
  • Originator control: access is scoped by the author (pseudonymous possible).

What is Churchen?

Churchen is the act of connection — the moment an idea becomes an address in the idea network.
A churcher only has to think; GIAP does the rest.

Why it matters

Today we filter for who, not for what. Many good ideas never reach the right minds in time. GIAP creates a neutral address space so ideas are discoverable and linkable from birth — independent of person or institution.

Example

A student in Nairobi imagines a new water-filter. Without GIAP: years of chance. With GIAP: seconds to engineers in Seattle, patent counsel in Zurich, sponsors in Singapore.
